Emergency Webserver

This is LoxBerry's Emergency Webserver. If you have tried to reach LoxBerry's normal webinterface but see this page, your LoxBerry has a severe problem and cannot provide his webinterface anymore... You can run the Healthcheck from here to figure out what's wrong and you can also reboot the system from here.
